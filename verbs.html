This is the page for understanding Russian Verbs

The verb is important to master since it gives us the ability to express actions to others. There are 2 main verb types in Russian.

  1. ать
  2. ить/еть

The first form will be expressed as following

Who Conjugation(change)
I/me y OR аю
You ешь OR ёшь
He/She ет
We(мы) ем OR ём
Y'all(вы)
