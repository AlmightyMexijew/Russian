The Alphabet

The Russian language uses the Cyrillic Alphabet. Some letters match the Latin alphabet (of English). KOMTA all sound the same.

Where it differs:

E is "yeh". B is "veh". ь is English "b". H is "N" sound. и is "ee...". See the table below


Ёё "yo" sound Ээ "ehh" sound Yy "oo"/"uu" sound. Ии "ee" sound
